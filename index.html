About Us

Superlative Steel Cast Pvt. Ltd. is a renowned manufacturer of top-quality Grey Iron Castings and S.G.I. (Ductile Iron) Castings. With a legacy of over 20 years in the foundry industry, our company is driven by a team of technically qualified and experienced professionals who are passionate about delivering perfection and excellence in every product we create.

With an ISO Certified facility boasting a manufacturing capacity of 6500 Metric Tons, we have the infrastructure and technology to produce castings ranging from 5 Kg to 70 Kg. Our state-of-the-art equipment includes induction melting, fully automatic green sand moulding with high-pressure line systems, and machining facilities.
